Arizona Winter School 2025: Representation theory of p-adic groups

AWS 2025 will be held March 8-12, 2025 at the University of Arizona in Tucson, AZ.

Schedule and other important information

Application/registration is required if you are planning to attend.

  • Registration is now closed.
  • The application/registration deadline for students was November 21th, 2024.
  • The application deadline for senior participants was December 15th, 2024.

All participants must abide by the AWS Code of Conduct and the University of Arizona nondiscrimination and anti-harassment policy.

  • Local directions
  • Organizers: Serin Hong, Hang Xue, Alina Bucur (main program) with Renee Bell, Brandon Levin, Padma Srinivasan, Anthony Várilly-Alvarado, Isabel Vogt, and David Zureick-Brown.
  • Funded by the National Science Foundation, and organized in partnership with the Clay Mathematics Institute.
